Welcome to Tome

Tome is a solution that directly addresses the problem of patients and other consumers from traveling between their home and their destination.

How does Tome work?

Providers like hospitals license our app and purchase Tome vehicles for customers. These providers then offer the service to customers as non-emergency transportation.

After pairing with your desired provider, you can request a vehicle at a certain time and date. After the request is approved, the vehicle will be delivered on the desired date.
